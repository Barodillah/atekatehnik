A Legacy of Industrial Precision

Founded in 2004, Ateka Tehnik began with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between traditional agricultural practices and modern industrial efficiency. Over the past 20 years, we have evolved from a local workshop into a nationally recognized leader in rice milling technology.

As an official LKPP vendor, we are honored to contribute directly to national food security initiatives. Our machines are not just equipment; they are the heart of milling operations across the archipelago, engineered to withstand the rigors of Indonesian climate and throughput requirements.
