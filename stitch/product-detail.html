Engineering Excellence

For over two decades, Ateka Tehnik has been at the forefront of agricultural engineering in Indonesia. The RMU Industri Menengah represents our commitment to bridging the gap between small-scale farming and massive industrial processing.

This unit is specifically engineered for medium-scale industrial production, offering a balance of high throughput and energy efficiency. We utilize high-grade manganese steel for all friction-heavy components, ensuring that your investment stands the test of time while maintaining the highest grain quality.

Our proprietary multi-stage polishing system ensures that the rice produced is not only clean but retains its nutritional integrity. The automation level allows for a single operator to manage the entire milling flow, drastically reducing labor costs while increasing output consistency.
